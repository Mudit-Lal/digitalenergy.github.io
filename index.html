Plaksha Global Conference on
Digital Transformation in Energy
28 Jan, 2020 | 3:30 PM

Reinventing Tech Education

Deep tech such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, big data, blockchain, advanced material science are changing the way we see and experience the world. Increasing adoption of these technologies also comes at a time when massive clean energy transition is underway. Recent profound changes in the way we produce, store and distribute energy, requires new set of technologies, policy framework, market design, business models, financial instruments and infrastructure to enable the transition and decarbonize the world. New developments in technology promise creation of a green, digitalized, decentralized and sustainable world.

Plaksha Centre for Clean Energy (PCCE) has been working at the confluence of interdisciplinary education, research and innovation, entrepreneurship and public-private partnership to champion efforts towards energy transition and global decarbonization.
